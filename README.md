# Naqvi Khan — Portfolio

I built my portfolio to be more than just a digital résumé — I wanted it to feel like a warm, personal space where I could share my journey. It's designed to be a clean, editorial-style experience that guides you through my work, my background, and the projects I'm currently pouring my energy into.

## Design & Aesthetics

- **A "Magazine" Feel** — I went for a look that's a bit more refined than a standard tech page, using lots of breathing room, elegant typography, and a subtle film-grain texture that just feels nice to look at.
- **"Liquid Glass" UI** — You'll notice the frosted, semi-transparent surfaces — I love how they catch the light and add a bit of depth to the page.
- **Day & Night Modes** — Depending on how your device is set, the site shifts between a warm, paper-like "Sand" theme and a deep, cozy "Dusk" theme. It's all about making it easy on the eyes.
- **Thoughtful Accents** — I picked a set of five pop-colors that I sprinkle throughout the site to keep things cohesive but lively, paired with soft, rounded buttons that feel great to hover over.

## Key Features

- **It Adapts to You** — The site automatically senses your system settings and swaps themes for you — no jarring flashes, just a smooth change.
- **Smart Navigation** — I made the navigation bar stick around when you need it and step out of the way when you're scrolling, so you can just focus on the content.
- **The "Feel"** — There's a bit of magic under the hood — the hero section subtly follows your cursor, and sections glide into view as you scroll.
- **Built with Care** — If you're someone who prefers less motion on your screen, the site respects your system settings and keeps the animations gentle.
- **A Little Personality** — Since I'm a programmer, I couldn't help but hide eight little "rubber duck" easter eggs around the site for you to find — it's a nod to one of my favorite debugging traditions.
- **Easy for Me, Better for You** — Because the site is built to be data-driven, I can update my latest projects or new experiences in seconds without having to break the site's layout.
